# AgentForge4j Brand Palette

Canonical mark: `agentforge4j-web-ui/public/brand/logo-horizontal.svg` (OSS repo, Apache-2.0),
mirrored byte-for-byte at `agentforge4j-docs/static/img/logo-horizontal.svg`. This is the single
source for both the `.org` site and the docs site — do not fork or re-derive it.

## Canonical Colors

- Primary: `#2d6ab2`
- Primary (shade): `#2a4a6e` — a secondary tone used internally within the mark, not a standalone brand action colour
- Secondary: `#1a202c` (dominant tone — wordmark and structural elements)
- Accent: `#3B82F6` (connector highlight)
- Foreground: `#FFFFFF`

## Rationale

Colours are the literal flat fills used in the canonical SVG — no other values are authoritative.
`#1a202c` is the file's most frequent fill (the wordmark and other structural dark elements).
`#3b82f6` is the brightest, second-most-frequent blue, used for the connector-node accents. `#2d6ab2`
and `#2a4a6e` are the two blues used within the "A" mark itself; `#2d6ab2` is the mark's dominant
blue and is designated Primary, `#2a4a6e` is its darker internal shade.
